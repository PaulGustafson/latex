\subsection*{Quantum Gates}
Not gate:
$$X = \begin{pmatrix}
  0 & 1 \\
  1 & 0 
\end{pmatrix}
$$

Hadamard gate:
$$ 
H = \frac {1}{\sqrt 2} \begin{pmatrix}
  1 & 1 \\
  1 & -1 
\end{pmatrix}
$$

Rotation gate:
$$ 
R_\theta = \begin{pmatrix}
  \cos(\theta) & \sin(\theta) \\
  -\sin(\theta) & \cos(\theta) 
\end{pmatrix}
$$

Pauli Matrices

$$
\begin{array}{ccc}
X = \sigma_X = \begin{pmatrix}
0 & 1 \\
1 & 0 
\end{pmatrix}
& 
Y = \sigma_y = \begin{pmatrix}
0 & -i \\
i & 0 
\end{pmatrix}
& 
Z = \sigma_Z = \begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 \\
0 & -1 
\end{pmatrix}
\end{array}
