\begin{theorem}[G - Runkel `17]
A factorizable pivotal finite tensor category admits a unique (up 
to scalar) modified trace on $\Proj(\C)$.

The pairing $\forall M \in \C, P \in \Proj(\C)$
$$\Hom_\C(M, P) \times \Hom(P, M) \to k$$
$$(f,g) \mapsto t_p(fg)$$
is nondegenerate. 
\end{theorem}
