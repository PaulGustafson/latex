\p{5.19} If $A \subset X$, then there is an exact sequence
$$ \cdots \to \tilde H_n(A) \to \tilde H_n(X) \to H_n(X,A) \to \tilde H_{n-1](A) \to \cdots,$$
which ends
$$\cdots \tilde H_0(A) \to \tilde H_0(X) \to H_0(X,A) \to 0.$$
(Hint: $\tilde S_*(X)/\tilde S_*(A) = S_*(X)/S_*(A)$.)

 $A \to X$ is an excision here; or use Theorem 4.13.)
