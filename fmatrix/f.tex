The fusion tensor, or $F$-move, for changing fusion bases has entries
\begin{equation}
\fusethreedirected{i}{j}{k}{m}{\ell} = \sum_n F^{i j m^*}_{k \ell^* n} 
\fusethreesdirected{i}{j}{k}{n}{\ell}
 \enspace .
\end{equation}
